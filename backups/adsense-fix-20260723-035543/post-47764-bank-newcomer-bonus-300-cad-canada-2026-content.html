Fine Print: How to Actually Claim Your $300 Bank Bonus

Every Canadian bank cash bonus for newcomers comes with qualifying conditions. Missing any one condition means forfeiting the full bonus amount. Here’s what to watch for:

  • Time limit to complete qualifying actions — usually 60–90 days from account opening. Mark your calendar.
  • Direct deposit requirement — must be an “eligible direct deposit” (employer payroll). Manual transfers from another bank often don’t count.
  • Minimum number of debit transactions — usually 5–10 debit purchases (not ATM withdrawals) within the qualifying period.
  • Eligible account types — some bonuses only apply to specific account tiers. Confirm before opening.
  • Residency requirement — you must be within 3–5 years of your Canadian arrival date. New arrivals qualify; long-term residents do not.
  • One bonus per household — if your spouse also opens an account, you both qualify individually.
