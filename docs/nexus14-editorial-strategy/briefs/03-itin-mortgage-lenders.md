## Angle

because they use different loan products entirely. Do not present one generic "best ITIN
mortgage lenders" list; a visa holder with an SSN-equivalent path should not read the same
recommendation as a no-FICO foreign national buying in cash-adjacent circumstances.

## Verified facts (as supplied — mortgage program terms change; re-verify lender specifics before publish)
- Guild Mortgage ITIN program: 15% down, 660+ credit score, up to $1.25M loan amount.
- United FCU: ITIN lending up to $1.5M.
- **FHA loans require an SSN** — ITIN-only buyers cannot use FHA; they need Non-QM (non-qualified
  mortgage) or portfolio lenders instead.
- Fannie Mae guideline B2-2-02 treats lawful permanent residents and lawful non-permanent
  residents the same as U.S. citizens for eligibility purposes (does not itself solve the
  ITIN-vs-SSN issue — cite precisely, don't overstate what B2-2-02 covers).
- Freshness/EEAT anchors: HUD 2025 guidance (HUD-25-048) + CFPB June 2026 ability-to-repay /
  immigration-status guidance — **near-absent from competitor content**, cite both explicitly,
  this is the freshness/EEAT differentiator called out in the brief.
